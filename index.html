About Us

Located in the charming town of Frostproof, Florida, the First Baptist Church stands as a beacon of faith and community. With a rich history and a strong commitment to spreading the message of Jesus Christ, this church has become a cornerstone for spiritual growth and connection in the area.

This church is dedicated to leading people to honor and glorify Jesus Christ by helping individuals from all backgrounds discover the joy of knowing and serving Him. The church's mission is built on a foundation of love, compassion, and service, making it a welcoming space for everyone.

The First Baptist Church of Frostproof, is more than just a place of worship; it is a vibrant community where people come together to grow in faith, support one another, and spread the love of Jesus Christ. Whether you are a long-time resident or just visiting, this church offers a warm welcome and a chance to connect with others who share your values.

Beliefs and Practice

At the core of the First Baptist Church of Frostproof are strong biblical beliefs centered on Jesus Christ. The church emphasizes the importance of worship, community, and personal growth through faith. Here are some key aspects of their beliefs and practices:

  • Biblical Focus: The church's teachings are deeply rooted in the Bible, providing a solid foundation for spiritual growth and understanding.

  • Worship Services: Sunday services are held at 11:00 AM, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere where attendees can engage with practical teachings from the Bible.

  • Family-Oriented: The church places a high value on family, providing children's ministries alongside regular services to ensure that all ages feel included and nurtured.

Leadership

The leadership at the First Baptist Church of Frostproof is dedicated to guiding the congregation with wisdom and compassion. Key leaders include:

  • Pastor Darrol Hood: Serving as the Senior Pastor since June 1, 2001, Pastor Hood is known for his strong preaching, leadership development, and counseling. His commitment to the church and its community is evident in his tireless efforts to inspire and support the congregation.

  • Matt Collins: As Associate Pastor and Music Minister, Pastor Collins plays a vital role in the church's leadership and music ministry. His contributions enrich the worship experience and foster a sense of unity among the congregation.

Community Engagement

The First Baptist Church of Frostproof is deeply involved in community activities, reflecting its commitment to spreading love and kindness beyond its walls. Some of the ways the church engages with the community include:

  • Outreach Activities: The church participates in local events like parades, using these opportunities to distribute materials and promote church activities. This outreach helps spread the message of God's love and invites others to join the community.

  • Community Support: Through various initiatives, the church supports local families and individuals, demonstrating its role as a caring and supportive neighbor.

Affiliations

The First Baptist Church of Frostproof is affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention, a network of Baptist churches that share common beliefs and values. This affiliation provides the church with resources and support to further its mission and outreach efforts.
